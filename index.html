GET CHARGED ONLY FOR
Viewed Flyers

With traditional flyers you pay for printing, transportation and distribution — and you can't change your design once printed. The biggest problem? You can't track if someone saw it even for half a second or scrapped it.

With Adviuz Digital Flyers, you don't pay for any digital distribution. You don't even pay if someone saw your flyer for less than 25 seconds. You only pay when someone has dedicated their full 25 seconds on your brochure.

If a viewer switches windows or tabs, the 25-second timer pauses — it only counts when they're actively focused on your flyer.
If someone views your flyer and sees it again within 24 hours — you are not charged again, regardless of how many times they revisit.

That's real people in your city spending 25+ seconds on your offer. Not impressions or clicks — actual engaged, reading viewers who stay long enough to take action.
